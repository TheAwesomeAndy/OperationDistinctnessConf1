## Writing strategy

Use a compounding gap:

1. EEG/ERP observations are noisy, subject-dependent, and partially observed projections of neural dynamics.
2. Endpoint accuracy does not characterize what temporal information a representation preserves, transforms, or loses.
3. Spiking reservoir encodings are often evaluated as classifiers rather than as temporal measurement operators.
4. Perturbation behavior under amplitude noise, temporal jitter, and channel dropout is underreported.

Every claim must pass:

1. Is it true under the measured protocol?
2. What technical decision does it justify?

Use refutable contributions, not generic roadmap language.

Avoid promotional wording such as:

* powerful,
* breakthrough,
* state-of-the-art,
* unlocks,
* revolutionary,
* solves.

Prefer:

* measures,
* characterizes,
* evaluates,
* bounds,
* preserves,
* suppresses,
* transforms,
* supports,
* fails to support.

## PCA protocol and language

The source audit found that the original reservoir embedding uses global pooled PCA:

* PCA is fit once, unsupervised and without labels.
* The basis is estimated over the full cohort.
* It is therefore transductive with respect to subject inclusion.
* It must not be called leakage-free preprocessing.

Fold-local PCA sensitivity was run:

| Representation             |     BA | Macro-F1 | Macro-OvR AUC |
| -------------------------- | -----: | -------: | ------------: |
| Fixed global pooled PCA A1 | 0.4626 |   0.4603 |        0.6440 |
| Fold-local PCA A1          | 0.4652 |   0.4619 |        0.6485 |

Required interpretation:

The fold-local sensitivity analysis did not show evidence of optimistic inflation from the transductive PCA basis under the tested subject-grouped protocol.

Do not say:

* “proves no leakage,”
* “leakage-free global PCA,”
* “PCA cannot leak because it is unsupervised.”

## Validation protocol

Preserve the source validation protocol exactly unless explicitly approved otherwise.

Default protocol:

* `StratifiedGroupKFold`,
* 5 folds,
* seeds `[42, 43, 44, 45, 46]`,
* subject IDs as groups,
* train-only standardization,
* balanced L2 logistic regression readout.

Do not silently replace the source protocol with:

* LOSO,
* GroupKFold,
* a different seed set,
* a different classifier,
* a different scaling protocol.

LOSO is a separate experiment and requires explicit approval.

ROC-AUC must be macro one-vs-rest AUC from pooled out-of-fold probabilities. If valid probabilities are not available, report BA and macro-F1 only.
