\caption{Operator view of affective-ERP representation evaluation. Trial-averaged scalp observations are treated as noisy measurements of an unobserved neural state trajectory, then mapped through fixed representation operators or a trained model. Channel loss is not a neutral evaluation step: zero fill and train-mean fill induce different standardized coordinates for non-centered features, whereas a centered reservoir embedding is nearly invariant to the fill convention. The primary comparison is therefore absolute balanced accuracy with subject-level uncertainty and paired encoder differences, with retention ratios treated only as secondary diagnostics.}
